## Project Description

Comprehensive school management system that modernizes educational administration through automation and centralization of academic and administrative processes.

### Key Features

- **Student and teacher management** with complete profiles
- **Grade recording** and automatic report card generation
- **Digital attendance tracking** with real-time reports
- **Parent portal** with access to academic information
- **Payment management** with payment gateway integration
- **Communications** through notifications and internal messaging
- **Automated** academic schedules and calendars
