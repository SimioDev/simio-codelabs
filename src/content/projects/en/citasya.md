## Project Description

Complete appointment management platform that allows service businesses to optimize their calendar and reduce no-shows through automatic reminders.

### Key Features

- **24/7 online bookings** with instant confirmation
- **Automatic reminders** via SMS and email
- **Intuitive admin panel** for appointment management
- **Synced calendar** with Google Calendar
- **Client management** with appointment history
- **Reports and analytics** on occupancy and no-shows
